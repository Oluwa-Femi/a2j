About Us

A2J was created with the aim of providing quality services in the fabrication and installation of stainless steel handrails and aluminium balustrade systems for homes and offices from its local production facility in Nigeria.

A2J Limited wide range of expertise in stainless steel fabrication has helped in providing solutions that answer the needs of today's clients.

With years of positive and value adding existence,A2J Limited has been at the forefront of distributiong bespoke stainless steel and aluminium balustrade system in Nigeria, providing industrial automation solutions and fabrication systems for organizations.

A2J Limited has continually shown itself to be the market leader through innovation and quality, delivering products that outperform and outlast the competition. Having gone through various levels of iteration, improvements and refinements. We balance creativity, reliability and combined skills necessary to envision, plan, design and manage our clients' projects with due consideration to budget and time.

Our steel specification is of the highest standard in the industry. Whether your choice is stainless steel or aluminium railings, we are committed to deliver world class stairs/railings that will match your taste. Our balustrades are durable, solid, reliable and possess the aesthetic ambience superior to competitors in the industry.

The activities of our company in the construction/interior industry covers domestic, commercial and government clients with eloquent testimonies to our dedication and deliveries in new buildings to renovations.

The customer lies at the heart of the A2J Limited product and service offerings, with the customer's needs and profitability placed at premium. A2J Limited products have been proven to have a very long lasting life span while guaranteeing a high level of maintainablility.
